PROJECTS BAYANIHAN

Riverville Fire Victims (August 2024)
Distribution of relief goods to the fire victims of Riverville Ups5, Barangay San Isidro.
Scotland Fire Victims (April 2024)
The goods may not be enough of what you have lost but on behalf of SK Council San Isidro, we hope this will bring little comfort to the fire Victims of Scotland.
Sitio Fatima Fire Victims (August 2024)
Distribution of relief goods to the fire victims of Sitio Fatima, Valley 6, Barangay San Isidro.
Lovewin Fire Victims (March 2024)
The San Isidro SK Council, led by SK Chairperson Kat Aragon and the SK Council, donated goods to the fire victims of Lovewin Compound. While the donations may not be sufficient to meet all the needs of the victims, what truly matters is the initiative to help and the thoughtfulness behind it.
Typhoon Kristine Victims (October 2024)
Earlier this morning, the SK Council of San Isidro, led by SK Chairperson Kat Aragon, along with SK Kagawads Gabriel Borja, Roland Detalo, and Jemimah Bañares, initiated a feeding program for the evacuees of Silverio Compound affected by Typhoon Kristine, held at the Silverio Covered Court.
