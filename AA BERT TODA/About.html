MISSION

To empower the youth of San Isidro by promoting leadership, active community participation, and creating opportunities for personal and social growth through inclusive and sustainable initiatives.

VISION

An integrated and progressive youth in San Isidro—led by leadership, innovation, and social responsibility—where all young people are motivated to make positive contributions towards society.

GOALS

YOUTH EMPOWERMENT

Create programs that build leadership, education, and personal development.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

Engage the youth in social, cultural, and environmental activities.

HEALTH & WELL-BEING

Foster mental health awareness, sports, and wellness programs for a healthier community.

SUSTAINABILITY & INNOVATION

Fund projects that promote economic opportunities and environmental sustainability.
